Phase 1 is here. We're inviting a small group of design partners to put
Ultralisk through their workloads before we open the doors wider.

## What's live

- **Models**: Llama 3.3 70B Instruct and Llama 3.1 8B Instruct, both served
  via `vLLM` on H100s with AWQ INT4 quantization. 128K context on both.
- **Endpoints**: Serverless (per-token) and Batch (50% off, 24h SLA). Both
  OpenAI-compatible — drop in the SDK you're already using.
- **Console**: Dashboard, Models, Playground, API Keys, Endpoints, Batch
  Jobs, and Billing. SSO and audit log are coming with the private deployment
  in Phase 3.
- **Pricing**: Token-priced, no seats, no commitments. See the full
  breakdown on the [Pricing page](/pricing).

## How to get a key

We're onboarding design partners one at a time. [Request access](/contact)
with a few sentences about your workload and we'll send you a key with
starting credit within a business day.

Direct email also works: `hello@ultralisk.io`.
